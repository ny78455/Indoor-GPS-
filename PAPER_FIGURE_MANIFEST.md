# Paper Figure Manifest

The runner supports storing digitized reference points under `experiments/paper_reproduction/reference_data/digitized/`; no digitized values are bundled in this repository. Digitized data must include method, estimated uncertainty, date, and whether collection was manual or automatic.

| Figure ID | Paper caption | x-axis / y-axis | Required module | Reproducible now? | Reason / script |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| To be populated from paper figures | — | — | — | No | The source paper’s numerical curves have not been digitized or supplied. Add one script per figure under `experiments/paper_reproduction/`. |

Do not embed copyrighted paper figures unless licensed. The dashboard/plots should overlay simulator output with digitized points and their uncertainty instead.
